The plot of Cyberpunk 2077 revolves around the hero/heroine V in the city of Night City: a dangerous and multicultural future megacity with powerful corporations, street gangs, violence, implants, and sex.

Corporations, also colloquially known as Corps: transnational companies, economic giants that can range from simple media corporations to global security firms, conducting their own policies, dominating all spheres of life on the planet, and often considered separate sovereign states.

Miletech , better known as "Militech" (English: Militech) — the largest megacorporation, one of the most influential companies in the world, specializing in the production and development of mid-priced combat firearms, military equipment and military transport for armed forces, private organizations, and local police forces, providing defensive and offensive security and military services, and developing and producing firearms, drones, combat implants, as well as military vehicles for land, sea, and air combat, supporting the U.S. federal government, recruiting soldiers for the army, and supplying technology, including weapons and specialized military equipment.

Arasaka (Japanese: アラサカ社) (Japanese: 荒坂社) - the oldest megacorporation on the planet, a world leader in corporate security, banking, and manufacturing, a giant arms dealer and manufacturer of high-tech weapons at the highest price category, the largest distributor of Japanese products in European, American, and Asian markets.
















Gangs and factions play a significant role, and their descriptions vary.

"Sixth Street"— a gang, a militarized alliance consisting of veterans of the Fourth Corporate War and recent wars, retired military personnel, former representatives of corporate security services and civilians with military training, the predominant gang in Arroyo and Rancho Coronado, Santo Domingo, Glen, and Vista del Rey, Heywood, as well as Charter Hill, Westbrook, Night City.

"Animals" — a gang of aggressive street fighters without a permanent territory, predominantly concentrated in the southern Pacific, working as bouncers and bodyguards throughout Night City.













"Maelstrom" — a gang of occultist mercenaries obsessed with cyber technology, viewing the human body solely as a platform for cyber modifications, one of the most dangerous criminal groups in Night City, the predominant gang in the industrial district of Northside and the north of Kabuki, Watson.





"Moxes" , also — a gang of sex workers, pimps, freaks, representatives of sexual minorities, social outcasts, punks, and anarchists, the youngest gang in the city, one of the predominant gangs in Kabuki, Watson, and Westbrook, Night City.










"Tyger Claws" (Japanese: 虎鉤衆) — a gang primarily consisting of Asians, using methods of organized Asian syndicates similar to triads and yakuza, one of the largest criminal groups in Night City, the predominant gang in Japan Town and Charter Hill, Westbrook, Kabuki, and Little China, Watson.






"Valentinos" , formerly — a gang mostly consisting of Latin Americans, representatives of Chicano culture in Night City and one of the largest gangs in the city, the predominant gang in Glen, Vista del Rey, and Wellsprings, Heywood.










"Voodoo Boys" — a gang of netrunners consisting of the diaspora of Haitian Creoles in the Pacific, the predominant gang on the Pacific coast.
