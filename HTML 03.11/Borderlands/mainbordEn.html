Weapons and loot

There are two new weapon manufacturers: COV and Atlas. Alternative fire modes for many types of weapons: from under-barrel attachments to dual weapon elements and much more. Most are limited to a few manufacturers: Maliwan, Vladof, Dahl, Atlas and Torgue. Manufacturers that do not have an alt-fire mode instead offer additional benefits such as frontal shields when sighted (Hyperion) and various reload and throw modules (Tediore) . This is limited to Hyperion, Jacobs, Tediore, and COV. Manufacturers now create their own parts for weapons, resulting in all weapons having unique parts depending on the weapon class and manufacturer. Common weapon names are divided by unique barrel type. All weapon classes have 3-4 variants, and not potentially more across all compatible manufacturers. All weapons have a listed "rating" in the top left corner of the item card, showing its overall effectiveness compared to other weapons. While they may be somewhat inaccurate, it allows players to quickly gauge a weapon's power at a glance. Higher grade weapons will emit longer, more visible beams of light on the ground, and purple and legendary tier weapons will make unique sounds to indicate extremely rare drops. Electronic weapons have been replaced with a unique "Eridian-fused" alien barrel attached to various weapons, causing the weapon to use extra ammo for guaranteed elemental damage and special effects such as continuous laser, bouncing shots or chain lightning elemental. Artifacts, Shields and Grenades more are not limited to one perk and can appear with several, for example, the Nova bonus and the Roid bonus for shields. Brushes, additional weapon bonuses that activate depending on various restrictions, can appear on any equipment and will begin to appear after completing story mode or purchased in the trade automatically. near Mad Earl.
